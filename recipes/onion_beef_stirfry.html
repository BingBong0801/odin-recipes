Onion Beef Stir Fry

Descriptions

Chinese Onion Beef Stir Fry is a flavorful and easy-to-make dish that combines tender strips of beef with garlic, onions, scallions, and a savory soy sauce-based sauce. The beef is sliced into thin strips and coated in cornstarch before being quickly stir-fried in a wok or skillet until browned and cooked through.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef flank steak, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup sliced onion
  • 1/2 cup sliced scallions (green onions)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Steamed rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together the beef strips and cornstarch until the beef is evenly coated.

  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add the beef strips to the pan and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes, until browned on all sides.

  3. Remove the beef from the pan and set it aside on a plate.

  4. Add the garlic, onion, and scallions to the pan and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes, until the vegetables are softened.

  5. Add the soy sauce, brown sugar, and sesame oil to the pan and stir to combine.

  6. Return the beef to the pan and stir-fry for another 1-2 minutes, until the beef is coated in the sauce and heated through.

  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  8. Serve the Chinese Onion Beef Stir Fry over steamed rice. Enjoy!
